Shipped Games

For five years, I worked full-time at Pixel Press in my hometown of St. Louis. While there, I helped ship four titles made with Unity. Being part of a small team meant my responsibilities were all over the map. I mainly worked on gameplay and rendering systems, iterating UX and game design, writing the camera "capture" interface for the Bloxels physical product, and general performance optimization.
